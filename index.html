Our Vision

Having each child in Community grow into a peaceful, hope filled and productive adult through emotional, social, skillful and spiritual support.

Our Mission

To promote physical and emotional wellbeing of children's healing through character education for children marginalized by Orphans, HIV/AIDS, War and Child abuse.

Our Comprehensive Objectives

Holistic approach to child development and community empowerment through 7 key pillars.

Education Access
Ensure quality education through school fees, materials, and career guidance for children aged 4-24
Holistic Development
Mentorship programs, workshops, and skill-building initiatives for youth development
Livelihood Improvement
Skills in soap making, goat rearing, poultry keeping, and agro-smart farming
Child Protection
Community work, hygiene practices, and child safety measures implementation
Emotional Support
Caregiver hub for emotional healing and mentorship training programs
Nutrition Support
Sack gardening and backyard farming for better child nutrition
Environmental Conservation
Go-green initiatives to improve community wealth and environment
